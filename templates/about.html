WHAT WE SEEK

THREE VISION MOVEMENT

Beloved Church strives to fulfill these three visions

based on the perfect words of Jesus

First, our church preaches according to what the Scripture says.

We constantly strive to honestly teach not the doctrines, ‘the commands of men,’ but the truth, ‘the commands of God’ exactly according to the Scripture without compromising it at all. (Mar. 7:1-9, 13; 2 Tim.2:15; Tit. 1:9)

Secondly, our church proclaims the gospel of the kingdom.

We guide our saints to the narrow path of life in order that they would possess heaven in their hearts and surely enter the kingdom of heaven as we proclaim ‘the gospel of the kingdom,’ not a false gospel taught by the Salvation Sect* by emphasizing repentance, the lordship of Jesus, living faith with works to follow, and the cost of being a disciple. (Mat. 7:21; Luk. 14:25-33; Act. 20:21)

*Salvation Sect: a Sandemanian heresy in Korea

Lastly, our church heals every disease and sickness.

We, clothed in the power of the Holy Spirit as the body of Jesus, do our utmost to deliver the city, the nation and the rest of the nations around the world by doing what Jesus did and even greater works. (Mar.16:15-20; Joh. 14:12; 1 Cor. 2:1-5)

In a word, our church carries out ‘the three-fold ministry’ (Mat. 4:23, 9:35) in the imitation of Jesus!
